About me

Hi, I'm Clément Peyrat and I'm 20. I've always been interested in technology and I knew I wanted to work in development.

That's why I attended LeWagon bootcamp to get as much knowledge on web development I could in a short time.

Other than that, I'm passionate about videogames, fantasy books, cryptocurrencies, fitness and common things like movies or series.
